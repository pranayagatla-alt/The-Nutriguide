ASTHMA

ASTHMA PRECAUTIONS:

To prevent asthma attacks, it is crucial to identify and avoid known triggers. Common asthma triggers include allergens like pollen, dust, and pet dander, as well as environmental pollutants such as smoke and fumes. Regular lung function testing can help monitor the effectiveness of medications and identify triggers. Developing an asthma plan with your doctor can help manage symptoms and track severity. Taking steps to avoid asthma triggers can help prevent an asthma attack.
